The Best Way to Play YSFlight.

YSFlight Reforged takes the beloved, lightweight YSFlight simulator and enhances it for modern systems. We've preserved the simple, fun gameplay while overhauling the engine for better performance, visuals, and quality-of-life features.

A note on the name: This project was originally started as 'CirnoYSF'. You may still see this name in filenames or version info. It's the same project, now reforged for a wider audience!

Key Upgrades

High-Performance Rendering

Experience ultra-smooth flight with high, stable FPS. Our optimizations make the sim feel incredibly responsive.

Enhanced Visuals

Fly through dynamic weather like rain and witness beautiful sunsets, bringing the world of YSFlight to life.

Simplified Setup

Get in the air faster. The built-in Auto Addon Installer and smarter defaults mean less configuration and more flying.

Actively Developed

We're passionate pilots and developers. Expect regular updates, bug fixes, and features requested by the community.

Vast Mod Support

Enjoy two decades of community-made aircraft, maps, and missions, all made easier to install and manage.
